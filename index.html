Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Northstar Medical covered by OHIP?

Northstar Medical is not currently covered by OHIP. Our services are offered on a fee-for-service basis. However, many patients find our pricing transparent and affordable — and some workplace benefits plans may cover virtual care visits.

How quickly can I see a doctor?

We offer same-day appointments for most services. After booking, you'll typically be connected with a licensed nurse practitioner within hours — sometimes sooner.

What if I need a prescription?

If clinically appropriate, your nurse practitioner can prescribe medication during your virtual visit. Prescriptions are sent electronically directly to your preferred pharmacy in Ontario.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. Northstar Medical is fully PHIPA compliant. All communications are encrypted and your health information is stored securely in accordance with Ontario's privacy legislation.

Can I cancel my Northstar+ subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your subscription stays active until the end of your current billing period.
